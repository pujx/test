Please be advised!

* To avoid your account being stopped out, we recommend you close all positions before submitting your transfer application;

* If your transfer-out account has open positions, or if the remaining margin level of your transfer-out account is higher than 300% after transferring funds, the system will automatically compete your transfer application;

* We allow transfer application for a trading account that has open positions. However, to ensure the security of the open positions of the transfer-out account, we will have the following restrictions:

   1: If your account's margin level becomes lower than 300% after the transfer, your request will not be processed;
   2: The formula of margin level calculation: (Equity – Credit)/Margin * 100%;

* If you participate in a credit bonus promotion, your bonus will be deducted accordingly.




If you trade in the transfer-out account and there is an unclosed position during the transfer, you will be responsible for the risks of the account being stopped out due to the transfer.

Withdrawal specification

Important Information

• GO Markets strictly prohibits third-party payments. All funds deposited into your trading account must come from a source that bears the account holder’s name. Equally, all withdrawals must be returned to a bank account in the same name as your GO Markets account due to third party transaction regulations.

• GO Markets will NOT be responsible for any delays/errors caused by payment systems, or caused by clients and/or a third party providing incorrect information.

• The name of the beneficiary bank account must be the same as the GO Markets trading account holder name. Otherwise, your withdrawal request will be rejected by GO Markets as a third party withdrawal.

• When you submit a withdrawal request, please make sure the amount of usable margin in your trading account is sufficient. Your withdrawal request will be revoked if the amount of your withdrawal request is inconsistent due to the fluctuation of your usable margin. If you have an open position in your trading account, be aware that the amount you withdraw may result in the closing of your position due to insufficient margin balance.

• If your remaining margin ratio after withdrawal is less than 300%, the withdrawal application will not be accepted.

Processing Timeframe

The withdrawal application for Live accounts normally will be processed within 1-2 working days.
